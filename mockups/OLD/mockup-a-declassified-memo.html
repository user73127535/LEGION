LEGION is a system for tracking the performance of groups of players who regularly participate in League of Legends matches together. Standard tracking platforms analyze individual performance. This system does not. All metrics are computed over games where two or more members of a registered group were present.

This system provides three primary outputs:

Output Description
Briefing Overview of cell activity and membership status
Field Report Win rates, champion synergies, and mode breakdowns for joint games
Operation Log Chronological record of all matches involving 2+ cell members
